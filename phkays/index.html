Our Code 08 and Code 10 training programs

Code 08
R6900

We offer a comprehensive Code 08 training program designed to prepare you for your learner's test with ease. Well assist you with your learner's book and provide unlimited lessons until youre fully confident and ready for your test. Our program is priced at R6900, with a R2500 deposit required before test booking. Join us, and we'll make sure you're fully prepared every step of the way!

Code 10
R6500

Our Code 10 training program ensures you're well-prepared for your learners test and beyond. We guide you in writing your learner's book and provide unlimited lessons until youre ready to take your test. The program costs R6500, with a R2500 deposit needed before booking your test. Get ready with us, and we'll support you until you're confident on the road!
